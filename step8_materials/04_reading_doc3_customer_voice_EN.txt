HALESTROM PILOT — CUSTOMER VOICE NOTES
Anonymised quotes from prospects who engaged with the pilot outreach
Sample of 12 across all three segments, varied stages and outcomes
Compiled by the Lisbon team


SEGMENT A — ENTERPRISE

E1 (closed-won, ~470k EUR ACV, financial services, 1,800 employees)
"What sold us in the end was the security review process. Your team had
SOC 2, ISO, the GDPR DPA ready, and the security questionnaire turnaround
was four days. Most vendors we evaluate take six weeks. That signals
seriousness. Frankly the product is good but not unique; the operational
maturity is what made the decision."

E2 (closed-won, ~520k EUR ACV, manufacturing, 4,200 employees)
"Our procurement process is brutal. The fact that your team had a master
service agreement template they could redline live with our legal team in
one session saved us probably three months. We've ditched vendors over
exactly this kind of friction before."

E3 (closed-lost, manufacturing, 900 employees)
"Honest feedback: the product impressed us, your sales process was tight,
but we couldn't get a price commitment beyond two years. Our finance team
needs five-year forecasts and you wouldn't quote past 24 months. We went
with [competitor] who would. If you'd flexed on that we'd be customers."

E4 (still in pipeline, healthcare, 2,100 employees, ~600k EUR potential)
"This is moving slowly because our internal change-management review is
slow, not because we have doubts. We expect to close this in Q1. Don't
take the silence as a no."


SEGMENT B — MID-MARKET

M1 (closed-won, ~58k EUR ACV, retail, 240 employees)
"Honestly the demo sold itself. Your competitor [Atlassian] is in our
stack already and we were looking for something simpler. The price was
right and the integrations covered what we needed. Quick decision."

M2 (closed-lost, agency, 180 employees)
"You came across as solid but we'd just signed with [Asana] three weeks
before your outreach. Bad timing. Re-engage us in 18 months when our
contract is up."

M3 (closed-won, manufacturing, 380 employees)
"Solid product, reasonable price. We're a Halestrom customer because we
were already using a basic version through a reseller; this was an upsell
more than a new acquisition. Your outreach happened to land at renewal."

M4 (closed-won, professional services, 95 employees)
"Switched from [Monday.com] mostly on price. Yours is roughly 30% cheaper
at our seat count and the feature gap was acceptable. Easy win for the
budget conversation."


SEGMENT C — SMB

S1 (closed-won, ~3.2k EUR ACV, agency, 22 employees)
"Loved the trial. The onboarding flow was the smoothest I've seen. Card
went in within a day. Whether we'll still be using it in 12 months is
another question; the team's already complaining the UI is too complex
for what we need."

S2 (closed-lost, e-commerce, 14 employees)
"Started the trial, never finished onboarding. We're using a free Notion
template instead. Your product is overkill for our team size."

S3 (closed-won, ~2.4k EUR ACV, design studio, 8 employees)
"Cheap, looks nice, does what we need. I won't lie, if you raise prices
even slightly we'll switch."

S4 (closed-lost, consultancy, 31 employees)
"Couldn't justify the per-seat cost. We're doing fine with shared
Trello boards."
