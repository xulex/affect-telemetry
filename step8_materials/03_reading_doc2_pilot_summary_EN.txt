WHAT WE DID

We ran a parallel outreach pilot across Halestrom's three target segments
to test segment responsiveness under a unified value proposition. Same core
message, same outbound cadence, roughly proportional spend per segment.
Pilot ran 13 weeks. Halestrom's existing sales motion (inbound + product
trials) continued in parallel and is not in scope of this analysis.

The full pilot data is in halestrom_pilot.xlsx. Below is a narrative read
of what the numbers show. Look at the data yourself; we have not made
recommendations in this document.


WHAT WE OBSERVED

Volume looked different across the three segments, as expected. SMB
generated the most leads (high inbound volume), Enterprise the fewest
(longer sales cycles, smaller addressable list). Mid-market sat in the
middle.

Conversion rates also differed. SMB had the highest top-of-funnel
engagement (opens, clicks, demo requests) but a notably lower close rate.
Enterprise had the lowest engagement metrics at every funnel stage but
higher close rates among those that did engage. Mid-market again sat in
the middle.

Deal sizes followed the expected ordering: Enterprise deals were
substantially larger than Mid-market, which were substantially larger
than SMB. This is consistent with the size of the buying organisations.

Sales cycle length also followed the expected ordering: Enterprise cycles
were the longest, SMB the shortest.

Customer acquisition cost (CAC) varied considerably across segments and
is one of the more important numbers in the spreadsheet. We have included
both blended CAC (total pilot spend / closed deals) and segment-specific
CAC (segment spend / segment closed deals).

A first read of "deals closed" alone tells one story. A read that
incorporates deal value and lifetime metrics tells a different one. We
encourage looking at both before forming a view.

NOTES AND CAVEATS

Pilot duration (13 weeks) is short for Enterprise sales cycles. Some
Enterprise opportunities are still in pipeline at pilot close. The deals
closed column reflects only what landed during the pilot window;
opportunities still active are counted separately.

Halestrom's existing customer base over-indexes on Mid-market. Some of
the Mid-market closes during the pilot may have been driven by existing
brand recognition rather than pilot-specific outreach. This is hard to
disentangle and we have not attempted to.

Anders Lindqvist (CEO) has been clear in steering meetings about his
preference for Mid-market and SMB. Carla has asked us to keep our
analysis independent of that prior. Please form your own view from the
data before reading Carla's memo.
